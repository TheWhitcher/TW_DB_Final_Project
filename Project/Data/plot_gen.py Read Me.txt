plot_gen.py Read Me:

How to generate a file:

1) Make sure prototype_csv.csv is in the same folder then plot_gen.py

2) Open the terminal and go to the root of plot_gen.py

3) In the terminal type or copy the following:
	python .\plot_gen.py

4) Enter one or multiple countries in the G20. Separate the countries with a comma followed by a space, and press enter. Exemple: Canada, France, Italy 

5) Enter the type of plot you would like to generate from the following:
	-Annual CO2 emissions 
      -CO2 Global Share
	-Annual methane emissions
	-Annual nitrous oxide emissions
	-Per Capita CO2
	-Carbon emission intensity of economies
	-Nitrous oxide per population 
	-Methane per population
